How the game works

Your Opponents cards:

You and your opponent will be assigned 5 random cards based on 5 randomly generated words. Keep in mind that there is a slight chance you and your opponent will receive fewer than 5 cards.

GO

When you hit GO, your cards and your opponent's cards are compared, and the player with the highest score wins.

Note: Due to a lack of available data, the card prices are fictitious.
